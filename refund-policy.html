Refund Policy

At AC CORP, we are committed to ensuring that our learners are fully satisfied with the courses they enroll in. However, if you are not satisfied, we offer the following refund policy:

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • If you request a refund within **14 days** of purchasing the course.
  • If the course has not been accessed or completed by the user.

2. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact us at contact@ac-corporations.com. Provide your account details, course name, and reason for the refund. We will review your request and respond accordingly.

3. Refund Timeframe

Once your refund request is approved, the refund will be processed within **5-7 business days**. Please note that refunds will be issued to the original method of payment (via Stripe).

4. Non-refundable Conditions

Refunds will not be granted in the following cases:

  • If the course has been completed or accessed (e.g., viewed more than 30% of the course content).
  • If the refund request is made after **14 days** from the purchase date.

5. Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and your continued use of the platform will constitute acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our refund policy, please contact us at contact@ac-corporations.com.
