Privacy Policy

At NeoAcademia, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit or use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide when you register, subscribe, or interact with our services. This may include:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, etc.)
  • Payment information (handled securely via Stripe)
  • Course progress and interactions with our platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process your payments securely
  • Communicate with you about courses, updates, and offers

3. Data Protection

We take your data security seriously and employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, we may share your information with trusted third parties such as:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe) for transaction purposes
  • Service providers that help us operate the platform

All third-party services are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies help us improve the functionality of our website and analyze web traffic. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us

If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at info@neoacademia.com.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated date will be indicated at the bottom of the policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@neoacademia.com.
